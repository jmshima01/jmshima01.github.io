Late Policy

You get 5 late passes by default throughout the semeter on any assignment. Without a late pass or an excused absence the following applies...

• 1 day late: -20% grade
• 2 days late: -40% grade
• 3 days late: -60% grade
• 4 or more days late: assignment is no longer accepted
