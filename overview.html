Our Mission Statement

"To create value and improve lives through sustainable and responsible mining."

Our Core Values

We take great pride in being a value-driven organization with a clear and compelling purpose. Our commitment to these five core values is foundational to how we operate our business, determine our operating locations, and guide our daily conduct:

  • Safety: The well-being of our employees, communities, and the environment is our top priority.
  • Inclusion: Embracing diversity and fostering an inclusive culture that respects and values differences.
  • Integrity: Upholding the highest standards of honesty, ethics, and accountability in all our interactions.
  • Responsibility: Recognizing our duty to act with care, considering the impact of our decisions on people, the environment, and society.
  • Transparency: As of 2023, we are proud to introduce transparency as one of our core values, recognizing its importance across all facets of our operations. Transparency guides our commitment to openness and honesty, influencing working conditions, environmental stewardship, investor relations, and more.

As the new CSR team of 2023, we are enthusiastic about this addition to our core values. Transparency is integral to our mission, ensuring that our operations align with our values, from creating safe working conditions to practicing environmental stewardship and fostering trust with our investors. We aspire to be an honest corporation dedicated to the betterment of the world, and transparency is the cornerstone of that commitment.
