Our Core Values

We take pride in being a value-driven organization with a clear purpose. Our five core values are fundamental to how we run our business, where we choose to operate and how we conduct ourselves every day:

  • Saftey
  • Inclusion
  • Integrity
  • Responsibility
  • Inclusion
  • Transparency

Moreover, as the new CSR team of 2023, we are beginning to implement transparency as one of our core values. Transparency is important in all our operations and impacts all our values from working conditions, to environmental stewardship, to investors. We strive to be an honest corporation working for the betterment of the world and to do so, we need to be transparent, the reason why we chose to make it one of our core values.
